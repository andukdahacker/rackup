========================================================
  RACKUP - Manual Game Script
  Print this out or pull it up on one phone.
  One person is the Referee. They run the game.
========================================================


BEFORE YOU START
----------------
1. Pick a REFEREE (they manage turns, read cards, track scores)
2. Everyone writes their name on the SCORE SHEET at the bottom
3. Each player secretly writes ONE custom punishment on a scrap
   of paper. Fold them up and toss them in a pile.
4. Pick your MODE (see below)
5. Decide on rounds: 5 (quick), 10 (standard), or 15 (marathon)
6. Play real pool. The script just adds chaos on top.


========================================================

  Streak tracker: put a tally next to their name for consecutive makes.
  Reset to 0 on a miss.


========================================================
MODE 1: PARTY MODE
"Real pool + chaos layer"
========================================================

EVERY TURN:
  1. Referee announces whose turn it is
  2. MISSION CHECK: Roll a die or flip a coin.
     - Die: 1 or 2 = secret mission (see mission list below)
     - Coin: heads = secret mission
     Whisper the mission ONLY to the shooter.
  3. Player shoots for real
  4. IF MADE:
     - +3 points base
     - Streak 2 = +1 bonus (4 total)
     - Streak 3 = +2 bonus (5 total) - announce "ON FIRE!"
     - Streak 4+ = +3 bonus (6 total) - announce "UNSTOPPABLE!"
     - If secret mission was completed, bonus points (see list)
  5. IF MISSED:
     - Streak resets to 0
     - Flip a coin: heads = they get an ITEM (see item list)
     - Draw a PUNISHMENT (see punishment list)
       * Rounds 1-3: use MILD punishments
       * Rounds 4-7: use MEDIUM punishments
       * Rounds 8+: use SPICY punishments
       * Randomly pull from the custom punishment pile too
  6. Update the leaderboard, announce standings dramatically

FINAL 3 ROUNDS: All points are TRIPLED! Announce this loudly.


========================================================
MODE 2: TOTAL CHAOS
"The app controls everything. The table is just a prop."
========================================================

EVERY TURN:
  1. Referee announces whose turn it is
  2. Referee picks a random CHAOS COMMAND (see list below).
     Read it out loud. The player MUST follow it.
  3. Also give them a random SECRET MISSION (see list)
  4. Player shoots under those conditions
  5. IF MADE:
     - Referee picks a random number 3-8. That's their points.
       (Chaos = random scoring!)
     - If mission completed too, add mission bonus
  6. IF MISSED - Referee picks ONE at random:
     - "SCORE SCRAMBLE!" - Pick a random number between 0 and
       their current score. That's their new score.
     - "POINT TRANSFER!" - They give 3 points to the player
       on their left
     - "PUNISHMENT TIME!" - Draw a punishment
     - "LUCKY MISS!" - They get 2 points anyway
     - "ITEM DROP!" - They get an item from the item list
     - "NOTHING!" - The chaos gods show mercy
  7. CHAOS EVENT (every 5th turn or whenever referee feels like it):
     - "SCORE SWAP!" - Highest and lowest players swap scores
     - "EVERYONE DRINK!" - Universal punishment
     - "DOUBLE NEXT!" - Next shot is worth double for everyone
     - "ITEM SHUFFLE!" - Everyone passes items to the left


========================================================
MODE 3: SABOTEUR
"One player secretly tries to lose"
========================================================

SETUP:
  - Fold up small papers: one says "SABOTEUR", rest say "INNOCENT"
  - Each player draws one secretly. DO NOT REVEAL.
  - The saboteur's goal: finish in the BOTTOM HALF of scores
    without getting caught

EVERY TURN:
  - Play like Party Mode (missions, items, punishments, scoring)
  - The saboteur must subtly play badly without being obvious

SUSPICION VOTE (every 3 rounds):
  1. Referee announces: "SUSPICION VOTE!"
  2. Everyone closes their eyes
  3. Referee says "Point at who you think is the saboteur"
  4. Everyone opens eyes. Count votes. Announce most suspected.
  5. No elimination yet - just building tension

EMERGENCY MEETING (can only happen ONCE per game):
  1. Any player can yell "EMERGENCY MEETING!"
  2. 60 seconds of open debate
  3. The accuser names their suspect
  4. Everyone votes: GUILTY or INNOCENT
  5. If majority guilty:
     - Correct? Accuser gets +15 points! Saboteur revealed!
     - Wrong? Accuser LOSES 10 points. Game continues.
  6. If majority innocent: meeting fails, game continues

END OF GAME:
  - Saboteur reveals themselves
  - If saboteur is in bottom half of scores: they WIN
    (even if someone else has more points!)
  - If saboteur is in top half: they were caught playing too well


========================================================
MODE 4: LOSER'S LEAGUE
"Everyone tries to MISS. Vote on who's faking."
========================================================

THE TWIST: You WANT to miss. But it has to look convincing.

EVERY TURN:
  1. Player takes their shot, "trying their best" (wink wink)
  2. IF THEY ACCIDENTALLY MAKE IT:
     - -3 points (making is BAD!)
     - Plus a punishment from the list
  3. IF THEY MISS:
     - Everyone votes: "CONVINCING" or "FAKING?"
     - If majority says CONVINCING:
       +3 points, plus +1 per convincing vote
     - If majority says FAKING:
       0 points and a punishment!
  4. Update scores

SUGGESTED EXCUSES (read one to the player before their shot):
  - "I was going for the bank shot..."
  - "The table is uneven!"
  - "Someone bumped me"
  - "I'm just warming up"
  - "That was my practice stroke!"
  - "This cue is warped"
  - "The chalk was bad"
  - "I sneezed internally"


========================================================
MODE 5: TEAM MODE
"Shared scores, shared glory, shared punishment"
========================================================

SETUP:
  - Split into 2 teams randomly (draw cards, odd/even, whatever)
  - Team scores = sum of all individual scores on that team

EVERY TURN:
  - Play like Party Mode but track team totals
  - On a miss, flip a coin:
    * Heads = TEAM PUNISHMENT (entire team does it!)
    * Tails = just that player gets punished
  - Secret missions benefit the whole team
    (each teammate gets half the bonus points, rounded up)

TEAM ITEM SHARING:
  - Players can gift items to teammates on their turn
  - Items used against opponents affect individual players


========================================================
MODE 6: QUICK ROUND
"5 rounds. That's it. Go fast."
========================================================

  - Always 5 rounds, no negotiation
  - No items, no missions, no chaos events
  - Just shoot and score:
    * Made = +5 points x current streak
      (1st make = 5, 2nd in a row = 10, 3rd = 15, etc.)
    * Miss = streak resets + a MILD punishment
  - Streaks are EVERYTHING in this mode
  - Fastest to play, perfect for "one more game" at last call

  Combo King            | Sink 2 balls in one turn                       | +8

--- ITEMS ---
(Write these on scraps of paper, draw from a hat)

  Item             | Effect
  -----------------|--------------------------------------------------
  Blue Shell       | Target 1st place! They shoot off-handed next turn
                   | AND lose 3 points
  Shield           | Block the next punishment or item against you
  Score Steal      | Steal 5 points from any player
  Streak Breaker   | Reset another player's streak to 0
  Double Up        | Next made shot is worth double
  Trap Card        | Next player to miss does YOUR punishment
  Reverse          | Swap your score with any other player
  Immunity         | Skip your next punishment
  Mulligan         | Redo your last shot (group votes to allow)
  Wildcard         | Invent any rule that lasts 3 turns

  ITEM RULES:
  - Max 2 items at a time. Discard one to pick up a new one.
  - Worse players get better items (rubber banding):
    * Last place: draw 2, keep the better one
    * Middle: draw 1
    * First place: draw 1 from a weaker pile (no Blue Shell)


--- PUNISHMENTS ---

  MILD (Rounds 1-3):
  [ ] Take a sip of your drink
  [ ] Compliment the person to your left
  [ ] Do your best impression of someone in the group
  [ ] Text the 5th person in your recents "I miss you"
  [ ] Speak in an accent for the next 2 turns
  [ ] Group picks a song, you dance for 10 seconds
  [ ] Give your phone to someone for 1 turn (they post 1 story)
  [ ] Say something nice about every player

  MEDIUM (Rounds 4-7):
  [ ] Shoot next turn with your eyes closed
  [ ] Group chooses your next drink order
  [ ] Call someone and sing happy birthday
  [ ] Do 10 pushups right now
  [ ] Shoot off-handed for 2 turns
  [ ] Let someone draw on your arm with a pen
  [ ] Speak only in questions for 3 turns
  [ ] Swap shirts with someone for the rest of the game
  [ ] Hold cue like guitar, play a solo before next shot
  [ ] Left-hand only for the rest of the round

  SPICY (Rounds 8+):
  [ ] Read your last 3 sent texts out loud
  [ ] Group composes and sends ONE text from your phone
  [ ] Post a selfie with a caption the group writes
  [ ] Call your most recent ex, ask about their day (speaker!)
  [ ] Karaoke one verse of the group's choice
  [ ] Group gets 15 seconds in your camera roll
  [ ] Venmo the current leader $1
  [ ] Worst player gives you a hairstyle for the night

  CUSTOM: Also shuffle in the punishments everyone wrote at
  the start! Pull from the custom pile whenever you want.


--- CHAOS COMMANDS (Total Chaos mode only) ---
  - SPEED ROUND: 5 seconds per shot, go go go!
  - MIRROR MATCH: Everyone shoots opposite-handed this round
  - SILENT ROUND: No talking, gestures only
  - BLINDFOLD: Close your eyes, another player aims for you
  - DOUBLE OR NOTHING: Hit = double points, miss = lose 5
  - CROWD CONTROL: Others vote which pocket you must aim for
  - MUSICAL CHAIRS: Rotate and inherit the next person's score!
  - BOUNTY: First to sink a ball gets 10 bonus points
  - THE FLOOR IS LAVA: Shoot without both feet on the ground
  - COMMENTARY: Player to your right narrates like a sportscaster
  - SLOW MOTION: Shoot and react in slow-mo, group judges it
  - TRICK SHOT: Must attempt a trick shot, group votes if it counts
  - ALLIANCE: Secretly pick a partner, both make = double points
  - CONFESSION: Confess something before your shot for bonus points
  - REMIX: Referee swaps two random players' scores!
  - RULE BREAK: Shooter invents 1 rule lasting 2 turns


========================================================
POST-GAME CEREMONY
========================================================

When the final round is done, the referee announces:

  1. "GAME OVER!" (dramatic pause)
  2. Read final scores from bottom to top
  3. Announce the podium: 3rd, 2nd, 1st with dramatic pauses

  AWARDS (pick all that apply):
  - MVP: Highest score
  - Sharpshooter: Best accuracy (makes / total shots)
  - Punching Bag: Most punishments received
  - Participant Trophy: Lowest score
  - Hot Hand: Longest streak of the night
  - Mission Master: Most missions completed
  - (Saboteur mode) Sneakiest: Saboteur, if they won

  4. Everyone roasts the Participant Trophy holder
  5. Winner picks the next game mode


========================================================
TIPS FOR THE REFEREE
========================================================

  - Be dramatic. Announce everything like a wrestling announcer.
  - Enforce punishments. No chickening out.
  - Escalate the spice level as the night goes on.
  - If the game drags, announce "SUDDEN DEATH: triple points!"
  - Keep the energy up between turns - trash talk is encouraged.
  - The ref gets to play too! Just have someone else ref your turn.
  - For missions: use the Notes app to text the player privately,
    or whisper it, or write it on a napkin and hand it to them.
  - For items: write them on scraps of paper and put them in a hat.
    Players draw from the hat when they earn one.
